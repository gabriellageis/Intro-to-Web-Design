CS Opportunities at NYU

NYU Courant

NYU Courant is part of the NYU College of Arts and Sciences (CAS). There are multiple STEM majors and minors at Courant specifically in the fields of Mathematics and Computer Science. There is also the Women in Science scholars program which is a group of high achieving women who want to pursue careers that are focused on research in math and science fields.

NYU Tandon

NYU Tandon is the engineering and applied science school at NYU. They offer many STEM majors and minors in all fields of Engineering as well as in Computer Science and Mathematics. They offer an accelerated bachelors/masters program which can be completed in 5 years.

STEM clubs for Women at NYU

STAC@Gallatin

STAC (Science, Technology, Arts and Creativity) is a new club at Gallatin that helps support and fund new innovative projects proposed by Gallatin students. These projects can be about anything as long as they take a creative approach towards science and tech.

WinC

NYU Women in Computing is a club at NYU that brings NYU students, faculty, and professionals together. This community holds talks with prominent female engineers and computer scientists from top companies in NYC. They also provide information about scholarships, research opportunities, internships, and jobs.

SWE

NYU Society of Women in Engineering is a club at NYU Tandon that provides guidance for the young women in engineering at NYU. They hold talks and workshops with local women in engineering. Their mission is to empower women to succeed in the field of engineering and they provide programs, scholarships, and networking opportunities for the young women in the club.
